Usage:
    /solve - Use this command to solve a differential equation.

Instructions:
1. Use the /solve command to initiate the solving process.
2. Follow the prompts to enter the order, differential equation, initial x and y values, point of approach, and step dimension.
3. The bot will apply the Runge-Kutta method to solve the provided differential equation.
4. View the results provided by the bot.

Example:
    /solve
    Order: Second
    Differential equation: -0.2*z-0.25*y
    Initial x value: 0
    Initial y values: 0.1 -0.1
    Point of approach: 30
    Step dimension: 0.2

Note:
- Ensure the provided differential equation is in the correct format.
- Follow the prompts carefully to input the required values.
